About This Project

This web application is the culmination of our final year project, in which we designed and implemented a cooperative meta-heuristic framework for the classic Traveling Salesman Problem. Users can launch exact and approximate solvers, visualize their convergence behavior, and directly compare multiple algorithms side by side. The goal was to create an interactive tool that demonstrates how genetic algorithms, ant colony optimization, simulated annealing, and other techniques perform on real-world TSP instances.
