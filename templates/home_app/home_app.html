About This Project

This web application is the culmination of our final-year project, in which we designed and implemented a cooperative meta-heuristic framework for the Travelling Salesman Problem. Rather than exact methods, it orchestrates and compares multiple meta-heuristics (genetic algorithms, ant colony optimization, simulated annealing, honey-bee colony, etc.) working in concert to explore and exploit different regions of the search space. Users can launch these cooperative solvers on real TSP instances, watch their convergence and error curves in real time, and directly compare how each strategy—and their combinations—perform side by side.
